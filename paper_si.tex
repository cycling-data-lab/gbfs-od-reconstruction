\begin{abstract}
This supplementary information accompanies the manuscript \emph{Standard
compliance bounds origin--destination identifiability in GBFS bike-sharing
feeds} \maintext{1--8}. Appendix~\ref{sec:si-A} proves the cost
identifiability theorem and the selection-bias decomposition;
Appendix~\ref{sec:si-B} proves the forward-stability lemma and the
sampling-horizon law; Appendix~\ref{sec:si-C} details the constant-estimation
procedures used in the pilot.
\end{abstract}
